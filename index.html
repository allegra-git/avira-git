Concept

Nothing is wasted.
Everything is made.

Avira begins where the vessel ends. Every rope, every teak plank, every off-cut of carbon — material with a history of wind, salt, and precision — is selected, stripped back, and reformed into objects designed for exceptional service.

The result is tableware and accessories that carry meaning before they are ever used. Pieces that belong on a table set for eight, passed by white-gloved hands, and noticed without knowing why.
